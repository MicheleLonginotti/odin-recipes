Pizza

Description

Pizza Margherita is a classic Italian dish that epitomizes simplicity and elegance in its flavors. It features a thin, crisp crust that serves as the perfect base for its limited yet flavorful toppings. The sauce is made from ripe, juicy tomatoes, often San Marzano, which are crushed and seasoned with a hint of salt and olive oil. This vibrant tomato sauce provides a fresh and slightly tangy contrast to the rich and creamy mozzarella cheese that is generously scattered across the pizza. Fresh basil leaves, added either before or after baking, lend a fragrant, herbaceous note that complements the other ingredients beautifully. A drizzle of high-quality extra virgin olive oil often completes the Margherita, adding a touch of richness and enhancing the flavors.

The origins of Pizza Margherita are steeped in Italian history, with the most popular story tracing its creation back to 1889 in Naples. It is said that the pizza was crafted in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy, with the ingredients chosen to represent the colors of the Italian flag: red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil. Today, Pizza Margherita remains one of the most beloved and iconic pizzas worldwide, celebrated for its simplicity, balance, and the way it allows the quality of its few ingredients to truly shine. Whether enjoyed in a rustic pizzeria in Naples or a modern eatery anywhere in the world, a well-made Margherita is a timeless culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (usually made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and a bit of olive oil)
  • Tomatoes (preferably San Marzano or another variety of ripe, juicy tomatoes)
  • Mozzarella cheese (fresh mozzarella, preferably made from buffalo milk or cow's milk)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Heat oven to its highest temperature (typically 475-500°F or 250°C).
  2. Shape Dough: Stretch or roll the dough into a thin circle.
  3. Assemble Pizza: Spread tomato sauce on dough. Add sliced fresh mozzarella and fresh basil leave
  4. Bake: Place pizza in the oven on a hot pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 7-10 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Finish: Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and serve immediately.
