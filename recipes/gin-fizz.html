Gin Fizz

Prep: 2:30 mins Shake: 15-30 secs Total: 3 mins Rating: 4.7 stars

A Gin Fizz is a refreshing and revitalizing cocktail that is perfect for warm weather or anytime you need a pick-me-up. Made with gin, lemon juice, and club soda, it is a light and fizzy drink with a hint of citrus. The lemon and gin balance beautifully, creating a crisp and clean taste that is sure to quench your thirst. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a meal, the Gin Fizz is a timeless classic cocktail that is sure to please.

The origins of the Gin Fizz are unclear, but it is believed to have originated in the United States in the late 19th or early 20th century. It is a variation on the classic Tom Collins cocktail, and its simplicity and refreshing qualities have made it a staple in many bars and restaurants. Today, the Gin Fizz is enjoyed by people all over the world, and its popularity shows no signs of slowing down.

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Simple syrup
  • Club soda
  • Lemon wheel or twist (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Fill Cocktail Shaker: Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add Gin and Lemon Juice: Add 2 oz of gin and 1 oz of fresh lemon juice to the shaker.
  3. Add Simple Syrup (optional): Add 1/2 oz of simple syrup if desired, to balance the acidity of the lemon juice.
  4. Shake: Shake vigorously for 15-30 seconds to combine and chill the ingredients.
  5. Serve: Strain the mixture into a chilled glass filled with ice. Top with club soda and garnish with a lemon wheel or twist.
